Each RNA command is encoded in a single line.  The first character of
the line is the symbol (see below).  The rest of the line is history
information, which is yet to be defined.


Symbol	Meaning

K	black
R	red
G	green
Y	yellow
B	blue
M	magenta
C	cyan
W	white
T	transparent
O	opaque

e	clear bucket

^	move
<	counter clockwise
>	clockwise
=	mark
-	line
!	fill
+	add bitmap
*	compose
&	clip

?XXXXXXX	dead rna
